Designer

I am a graphic designer based in Ottawa, Canada. I specialise in logo design, document layout and identity. I create beautiful, usable, professional logos and illustrations. All my work is built from hand drawn designs. For creating my pieces my weapons of choice are Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign from Adobe. Currently, I am in my second year of the challenging three year Graphic Design course at Algonquin College. They've strengthened me through training in typography, the basic fundamentals of creativity, design, image. When I am not designing, I am enjoying music with my boyfriend of 5 years. This is all I have ever wanted to do, my dream job. I am currently available for freelance work.
